Whimsy Works began with a handful of cosplayers at the Toronto Children’s Book Bank and has since grown into a province-wide collective of artists and volunteers. Our team handmakes and maintain dozens of size-inclusive, theme-park quality costumes, and we work with charitable organizations, non-profits, and families across Ontario.

Whimsy Works is entirely volunteer-run. 85% of our visits are dedicated to charitable work and non-profit partnerships. By choosing us for your private events, you are directly providing accessible, high-quality experiences for families and organizations across Ontario.

Making Magic Across Ontario

Taylor Swift was one of twenty characters from Whimsy Works to join Starlight Children's Foundation at their 2025 Trick or Suite event, an annual accessible halloween event in Aurora.

Elsa visited the children's cancer wing of Trinity Memorial Hospital in Port Credit, in honor of Children's Cancer Awareness month in 2024.

Elsa, Anna, and Tinkerbelle brought some fairytale magic to the annual Walk to Change Cystic Fibrosis History, hosted by CF Canada in 2025.
