Become a Volunteer

Join our team of magical performers and make a real difference in children's lives.

Why Volunteer With Us?

Whimsy Works is a volunteer-run nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing high-end, theme-park quality character experiences to pediatric hospitals, fundraisers, and community events at absolutely no cost. Our volunteers are the heart of everything we do.

Make a Difference

Bring joy and magic to children who need it most, including those in pediatric hospitals and underserved communities.

Join a Community

Become part of a supportive team of passionate performers who share your love of characters and giving back.

Develop Skills

Gain experience in performance, character work, event coordination, and nonprofit operations.

Flexible Schedule

Volunteer on your own time. We work around your availability and never require a minimum commitment.

What We're Looking For

Performers

Bring characters to life! We need performers who can embody princesses, superheroes, and other beloved characters with authenticity and warmth.

Costume & Makeup Artists

Help create the magic behind the scenes with costume maintenance, wig styling, and character makeup.

Event Support

Assist with event logistics, photography, social media, and ensuring everything runs smoothly.

Administrative Help

Support our operations with scheduling, communications, fundraising, and organizational tasks.

Ready to Join?

We'd love to hear from you! Send us an email telling us a bit about yourself, what roles interest you, and why you'd like to volunteer with Whimsy Works.
