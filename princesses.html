The Snow Queen

Elsa

Elsa inspires children to embrace what makes them different and to be brave enough to be themselves — and she proves it's even more fun when you've got ice powers. With her regal presence and magical storytelling, she captivates children of all ages and creates moments they'll talk about long after the party is over.

Outfits

❄️ For winter outdoor bookings, the Christmas and Travel outfits are strongly recommended.

Games

  • ❄️ Freeze! — children practice their ice powers
  • ⛄ Best snowman storytelling contest
  • 🌀 Pass the Snowflake (musical chairs variant)

Songs

  • 🎵 Let It Go
  • 🎵 Into the Unknown
  • 🎵 Show Yourself

The Princess of Arendelle

Anna

Brave, optimistic, and full of heart, Anna brings boundless warmth and infectious energy to every gathering. She teaches children about the power of love, determination, and never giving up on the people you care about — all while handing out the biggest hugs in Arendelle. Her cheerful, silly spirit lights up every room she walks into.

Games

  • 🤗 Biggest hug in Arendelle contest

The Little Mermaid

Ariel

Adventurous, curious, and endlessly enchanted by the human world, Ariel brings the magic of the ocean to every gathering. She loves sharing her collection of treasures, swooning over gadgets and gizmos, and reminding children that following your dreams — even the wildest ones — is always worth it. Full of wonder and warmth, she's an absolute delight for little dreamers and ocean lovers alike.
